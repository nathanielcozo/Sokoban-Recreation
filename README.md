## Goal
The primary goal of the Sokoban game is to act as a warehouse keeper (the player token) and push all the boxes on a puzzle level onto designated storage locations

## Key Objectives and Rules:
- Push boxes: The player must push boxes to target spots
- No pulling: Boxes can only be pushed, not pulled
- Limited movement: The player cannot walk through walls or push multiple boxes at once
- Completion: The level is successfully completed when every box is positioned on a goal spot
- Avoid deadlocks: The player must move strategically to avoid trapping boxes in corners or against walls, which makes the puzzle unsolvable

## Controls:
- W: Walk upwards
- S: Walk downwards
- A: Walk to the left
- D: Walk to the right
- Restart: Restarts the game
- Quit: Exits the game
